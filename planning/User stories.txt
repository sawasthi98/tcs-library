**Unregistered**
As an unregistered user when I,
Click log in it takes me to a log in page.
Log in it tells me to create an account.
Click sign up it takes me to a registry page.


**Logged out**
As a logged out user when I,
Click sign up it takes to me a register page.
Click log in it takes me to a log in page.
Click search for an item, it takes me to a log in page.
Click My profile it takes me to a log in page

**Logged in**
As a logged in user when I,
No longer see sign up or sign in links.
Click search for a book and it takes me to a book search list.
Click my profile and it takes me to a user data page with a "bookshelf"
